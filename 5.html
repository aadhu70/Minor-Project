XBOX Series S

The Xbox Series S is a digital-only console with a compact design that delivers high performance and great value. It comes with a 512GB SSD and 10GB RAM for quick load times and smooth gameplay.

With support for 1440p gaming at up to 120fps, the Series S provides excellent visuals for both gaming and streaming content. It also supports ray tracing, HDR, and variable rate shading for enhanced graphics.

Backward compatibility allows for playing thousands of games from the previous generations, and Xbox Game Pass provides access to a vast library of games for a monthly fee. The console also supports Xbox Smart Delivery, which upgrades games to their optimized version for the Series S.
