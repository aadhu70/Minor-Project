PS4 Pro

The PS4 Pro is an upgraded version of the original PS4, released in 2016. It features improved hardware, including a faster CPU and GPU, which allows for 4K gaming and improved performance in some games. Additionally, the console includes features such as HDR support and a larger hard drive capacity, making it a great choice for gamers who want the best possible experience on their TV.

The PS4 Pro also includes features such as improved frame rates and faster load times. This can be particularly noticeable in games that are optimized for the console, where players can enjoy a smoother and more responsive gameplay experience..

One of the standout features of the PS4 Pro is its ability to play games in 4K resolution. This provides gamers with a much more immersive experience, with more detailed graphics and improved image quality. However, not all games are compatible with 4K, and some may require updates to take advantage of the improved hardware.
