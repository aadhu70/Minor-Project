XBOX Series X

The Xbox Series X is the latest gaming console from Microsoft with faster loading times, ray tracing, and 4K resolution at 60 frames per second.

With a custom-designed processor, this console is built to handle demanding games with ease. Its SSD storage allows for faster game loading and switching, and its backward compatibility ensures that you can play your favorite Xbox games from previous generations.

The Xbox Series X also features a Quick Resume feature, allowing you to switch between multiple games without having to wait for them to load. Additionally, the console's Smart Delivery system ensures that you always play the best version of a game on your console, regardless of whether you own the original or an upgraded version.
